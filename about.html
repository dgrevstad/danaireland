About Me

I'm a product designer and developer that specializes in accessibility. I work with federal agencies to ensure their Information Communications Technology (ICT) meets Section 508 standards.


My current work is in digital content strategy and website redesign for colleges & universities. Previously I worked for the University of Minnesota doing UX testing and recruitment. I also work in UX/UI design, and you can access my current visual design porfolio here.


If you are interested in accessibility or usability testing, UX consulting, or if you just want to say hi, you can reach me via email.


When I'm not working, I enjoy reading, gardening, working out, and playing with my dogs.
